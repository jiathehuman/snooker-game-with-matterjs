Controls --------------------------------------------------

To move the cue's direction hover around with your mouse.
To release cue and hit cue ball: Mouse-click and mouse-release.
To change force of cue Adjust slider above.
To place the cue-ball in desired location: Hover to space you want to place ball and press "a" on keyboard. Notice that it does not work if you are near other balls.

  • Choose Game Mode One for the classic snooker experience!
  • Choose Game Mode Two for practice! Only red balls will be generated.
  • Choose Game Mode Three for all balls to be in random locations!
